About Me.

Hello, I'm Aman Sharma, an MBA student specializing in Finance at Sri Sri University. In 2021, I founded "Just a Helper," an NGO based in Siliguri dedicated to community support and development. My journey has shaped me into a strategic innovator and visionary leader, committed to making a positive impact both in business and society. I have a diverse professional background, having worked as an HR Manager and in the BPO sector. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Swami Vivekananda Subharti University in Meerut, which has given me a strong analytical foundation.

I possess a range of skills, including corporate finance, financial analysis, banking operations, and human resources. My experience has also strengthened my interpersonal and communication abilities, making me effective in interviewing and team collaboration.

Additionally, I hold three certifications: one from HP Life in cash flow management, another from Tata Consultancy in financial accounting and managerial finance, and a third in cybersecurity from Tech Mahindra. I am passionate about continuous learning and look forward to utilizing my knowledge and skills to drive meaningful change.
